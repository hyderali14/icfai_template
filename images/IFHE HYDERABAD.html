IFHE HYDERABAD

The ICFAI Foundation for Higher Education is declared as a Deemed to be University, under Section 3 of UGC Act. 1956. It has evolved a comprehensive student-centric learning approach consisting of several stages, designed to add significant values to the learner's understanding in an integrated manner, covering relevant knowledge, practical skills and positive attitudes. The IFHE is a member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) and Association of Commonwealth Universities (ACU).
